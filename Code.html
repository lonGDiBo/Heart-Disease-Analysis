Tóm tắt¶

  • T lu bnh tim c mi ngi quan tm, c rt nhiu cuc nghin cu v bnh tim c thc hin. X hi ngy cng pht trin gip cht lng cuc sng ca con ngi c nng cao. Th nhng mt tri ca s pht trin ny li li h ly mi trng v sc khe con ngi. Trong , cc bnh lin quan ti tim mch ngy cng nhiu v li hu qu ng tic. Tuy nhin vi s tin b ca y hc tm ra nhiu phng php chn on, iu tr hiu qu hn, hin i hn gip phn no gim ni lo v bnh tim.
  • Chnh v th nhm chng em chn la chn ti D on cc kh nng mc bnh tim thng qua tp d liu cho trc c th hiu su hn v tng hp li cc triu chng v biu hin thng gp ca ngi mc bnh tim. Qua c th phn no phng trnh cho chnh mnh v cho nhng ngi xung quanh.
  • Bi ton thuc prediction nn nhm s dng thut ton hi quy logictic phn tch v a ra nhng d on ph hp gii quyt cc cu hi m nhm t ra.

Giới thiệu¶

  • Theo thng k, trc y bnh tim thng ch xut hin ngi cao tui nhng hin nay tui mc bnh tim ngy cng tr ha. Cng vi , c rt nhiu nguyn nhn gy ra kh nng mc bnh tim nh tui tc, gii tnh, huyt p khng n nh, lng m trong mu,... v ngi mc bnh tim c cc biu hin khc nhau nn vic pht hin sm v ngn chn l iu v cng cn thit. in tm l th ghi li hot ng ca tim nn n l c s quan trng c th pht hin nhng yu t bt thng ca tim c nguy c dn n bnh. T , nhm a ra cc cu hi cn phi c lm r nh sau:
    • Nhng yu t no cho thy r kh nng mc bnh tim?
    • tui c nh hng n nguy c mc bnh tim hay khng?
    • Trng thi in tm ca ngi mc bnh tim c g bt thng?
    • C s khc bit v nguy c mc bnh tim gia nam v n hay khng?
  • tr li cc cu hi trn bi ton s dng tp cc bin gii thch nh: tui, gii tnh, huyt p, nhp tim,... v thut ton logistic regression d on kh nng mc bnh tim

Dữ liệu¶

  • Tp d liu phn tch c ly t https://www.kaggle.com/ v c t nm 1988. B d liu ny c t nm 1988 v bao gm bn c s d liu: Cleveland, Hungary, Thy S v Long Beach V. N cha 76 thuc tnh, bao gm thuc tnh d on, nhng tt c cc th nghim c cng b u cp n vic s dng mt tp hp con gm 14 trong s . Trng "mc tiu" cp n s hin din ca bnh tim bnh nhn. N c gi tr s nguyn 0 = khng c bnh v 1 = bnh.
  • Trong qu trnh nghin cu c th xy ra cc trng hp:
    • S cc ca d on mc bnh v thc t b mc bnh tht.
    • S cc ca d on khng mc bnh v thc t khng mc bnh tht.
    • S cc ca d on mc bnh v thc t khng b mc bnh.
    • S cc ca d on khng mc bnh v thc t b mc bnh.
  • Cc thuc tnh:
    • age: tui tnh theo nm
    • sex: gii tnh
    • cp - 'check_pain_type': cc loi au tht ngc
    • trestbps - 'resting_bool_pressure': huyt p lc ngh (mm Hg)
    • chol - 'cholesterol': cholestoral trong huyt thanh tnh bng (mg / dl)
    • fbs - 'fasting_blood_sugar': ng huyt lc i
    • restecg - 'rest_electrocardiographic': kt qu in tm lc ngh ngi
    • thalach: nhp tim ti a t c (BPM: nhp trn pht)
    • exang - 'exercise_included_angina': au tht ngc do vn ng (ch xy ra do vn ng)
    • oldpeak: chnh lch ca on ST gy ra do tp th dc lin quan n ngh ngi (mm)
    • slope - 'ST-slope': dc ca on ST tp th dc nh cao
    • ca: s lng mch chnh quan st c khi quang ni soi bng hunh quang
    • tha - 'thalassemia': bnh tan mu bm sinh
    • target: chn on bnh tim
